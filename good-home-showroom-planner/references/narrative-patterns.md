# Narrative Patterns

## Standard Good-Home Evidence Chain

Use for most residential sales centers:

品牌/政策背书 -> 好房子总论 -> 安全系统 -> 舒适健康系统 -> 绿色低碳系统 -> 智慧系统 -> 精工工法验证 -> 服务闭环 -> 洽谈/样板间导流

Best when the client needs a comprehensive product-power hall.

## Compact Conversion Route

Use when area is small or sales conversion is the main goal:

入口精神锚点 -> 居住痛点共鸣 -> 系统答案总览 -> 核心体验展项 -> 技术证据链 -> 落座定制/沟通 -> 服务收束

Design rules:

- Use a forced route if the story depends on sequence.
- Put the hero experience around the midpoint, after pain is established.
- Put seating after evidence, not before evidence.
- Give sales a natural reason to move from standing explanation to seated discussion.

## Luxury Long-Life Route

Use for high-end riverfront, mansion, branded residence, or long-life value projects:

在地居住命题 -> 长期价值标准 -> 工法实证 -> 日常体验兑现 -> 长期托付

Good proposition types:

- 城市中心: 外繁内静、把城市留在门外。
- 江岸/海岸: 不止风景，也要回应风、光、声、湿、潮、防水、维护。
- 北方寒冷: 长冬舒适、稳定供热、空气湿度、静音与设备可靠。
- 南方湿热: 防潮防霉、热湿平衡、遮阳隔热、空气品质。
- 豪宅改善: 家族长期主义、资产稳定、体面秩序、隐形系统。

## Spatial Zone Set

Choose from these zones and rename them to fit the project:

- 门头/入口: Establish arrival and theme.
- 序厅: Brand authority, policy context, project thesis.
- 痛点廊: Make customers feel the problem before the answer.
- 总览区: Show the system map, not too much detail.
- 主题系统区: Good community, good product, good service, or safety/comfort/green/smart.
- 英雄体验区: Quiet room, acoustic cabin, water/air lab, energy model, smart scenario, craft test.
- 工法长廊: Hidden engineering evidence.
- 高定/定制/洽谈区: Convert belief into personal choice.
- 服务闭环区: Delivery, warranty, maintenance, property service, FAQ.
- 结语区: Memory sentence and sample-room linkage.

## Visitor Timing

- 15 minutes: one thesis, four proof points, one hero experience, one service closure.
- 25 minutes: pain -> system -> 2-3 hero exhibits -> seated conversion.
- 45 minutes: full hall + sample-room linkage + FAQ/price support.
