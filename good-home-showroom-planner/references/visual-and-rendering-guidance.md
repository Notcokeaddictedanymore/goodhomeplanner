# Visual And Rendering Guidance

## Preferred Atmosphere

For luxury good-home showrooms, prefer:

- Warm stone, limewash, travertine texture, warm beige, champagne metal, bronze, dark grey stone, low-glare glass.
- Low illuminance, high CRI, 2700K-3000K warm light, precise accent lighting.
- Embedded exhibits, recessed niches, controlled wall text, refined display cases, concealed wiring.
- Quiet technology: screens integrated into walls, low-saturation UI, no aggressive blue HUD.
- Real samples and section models treated like luxury objects, not trade-show props.

Avoid:

- Generic technology blue, full-wall screens everywhere, crowded parameter boards, loud sci-fi UI, exhibition-booth truss language, excessive slogans, overdecorated gold.

## Rendering Prompt Pattern

Use this structure for image-generation or rendering briefs:

Project type + zone + spatial composition + exhibit elements + materials + lighting + graphic language + camera + exclusions.

Example:

高端住宅“好房子生活馆”静音舱入口区，强制单动线转折空间，暖米艺术漆弧形墙、香槟金属窄边、深灰半哑光石材地面、嵌入式分贝屏、系统窗剖面样件、低反射玻璃展柜、2700K线性洗墙光，黑金图文极简排版，酒店式安静书房氛围，24mm广角，真实室内摄影质感，避免科技蓝、避免展会感、避免拥挤文字。

## Zone Prompt Starters

- 序厅: 精神入口、书页式形象墙、主题金属字、少量关键词、到达感。
- 痛点廊: 低照度窄幅灯箱、生活问题标本、抽象声波/气候/归家线图。
- 工法长廊: 样件托台、剖面模型、时间轴、单品打光、错误/标准做法对比。
- 静音舱: 盒中盒、安静书房、分贝可视化、声源路径图、吸音材料。
- 技术中枢: 净水/新风/暖通/智能样件、系统关系屏、透明模型。
- 高定定制区: 弧形沙发、材料样板桌、互动屏、圆形地毯、精品酒店书房感。
- 服务收束区: 时间轴、服务图谱、轻量屏幕、温暖结语。

## Design QA

Check that:

- The first view tells the project identity, not just the brand logo.
- Text remains sparse and legible.
- Samples are reachable, lit, and maintainable.
- The route has rhythm: narrow/wide, dark/light, standing/seated, proof/emotion.
- The hero exhibit has enough acoustic, mechanical, electrical, and operational feasibility.
